The Journey

Born and raised within a small town in Georgia and gifted with a powerful drive and an inquisitive mindset. Not knowing where these gifts would lead me, I pursued many interests until fate lead me to Computers. My first experience was with Game Development and unfortunately due to my lack of experience and knowledge, I failed. Unknowingly, I found this failure as the spark needed to ignite the passion I now have for Computer Science. I worked for 1yr independently on a simple racing game with no computer experience or a firm understanding of the fundamental concepts of any subjects involved. Nearing completion the computer I was working on failed on me and all progression was lost. I was down but not out.

Currently a Chemistry Major my I could not get my mind off of the outcome of the project. Not because it failed but what could I have done to better my experience. One day I met a Computer Science Professor, Denis Masters whom open the door to the answers I seeked. When we first met I talked about my project and the objectives that I wanted to accomplish. He spoke of the wonders of Computer Science and with my drive I would find the correct path. This inspired me, I Changed my major and the real journey began.
